### TeenPopTastic Privacy Policy

**Unique Data Collection:**
- Music listening history (from Spotify/Apple Music)
- Music preferences (artists, genres)
- Vibe matches and compatibility scores
- Music moments (photos/videos with songs)
- Group vibe participation
- Date of birth (for age verification)

**How We Use This Data:**
- Match users with compatible music tastes
- Generate vibe compatibility scores
- Facilitate social connections
- Curate music discovery
- Enforce age-based safety features

**Unique Sharing:**
- Your music profile is visible to matched users
- Your music moments are visible to friends
- Vibe compatibility scores are visible to mutual matches
- **NEVER shared with third parties for advertising**

**COPPA Compliance Section:**

```
## 6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

### Age Requirements
- Under 13: NOT ALLOWED
- Ages 13-15: Parent email verification REQUIRED
- Ages 16-17: Full access with location sharing OFF by default
- Ages 18+: Full access

### Parental Controls
Parents of users aged 13-15 can:
- Request access to child's account data
- View child's connections (not message content)
- Delete child's account
- Contact safety@joinvibe.app for concerns

### Under 13 Detection
If we discover a user is under 13:
- Account will be immediately terminated
- All data will be deleted within 10 business days
- Parent will be notified if email was provided

### Data Minimization for Teens
We limit data collection for users under 16:
- Location: City-level only
- Listening activity: OFF by default
- Profile visibility: Friends-only by default
```

**Safety Features:**
- Content moderation via AI
- Report and block functionality
- Crisis resources access
- Parent notification for policy violations
