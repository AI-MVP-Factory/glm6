## Launch Checklist

### Pre-Launch (Week -4 to -1)

- [ ] App icons designed and approved
- [ ] Screenshots captured and edited
- [ ] Preview videos produced
- [ ] Promotional text finalized
- [ ] App Store descriptions finalized
- [ ] Keywords researched and finalized
- [ ] Social media accounts created
- [ ] Landing page live with email capture
- [ ] Press kit assembled

### Launch Week

- [ ] Product Hunt listing scheduled
- [ ] Social media content scheduled
- [ ] Email to waitlist
- [ ] Press outreach (targeted list)
- [ ] Community engagement (Reddit, Discord, etc.)
- [ ] Influencer outreach (if applicable)

### Post-Launch (Week +1 to +4)

- [ ] Monitor App Store reviews and respond
- [ ] Track download metrics daily
- [ ] Engage with social media mentions
- [ ] Share user-generated content
- [ ] Send weekly update emails
- [ ] Iterate based on feedback
